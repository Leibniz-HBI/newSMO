As part of our efforts to collect lists of "public speakers" and their Social Media accounts, we collected a list of all candidates running for the Federal Election 2021 in Germany ("Bundestagswahl"). 

<!--more-->

This list is now available for download and further analysis:

Schmidt, Jan-Hinrik, Philipp Keßling, Jan Rau, Clara Linnekugel, Jasmina Moradi, Fred Nasser (2022): Twitter- und Facebook-Accounts der Kandidierenden zur Bundestagswahl 2021. Online verfügbar: [https://doi.org/10.17605/OSF.IO/WN48Y](https://doi.org/10.17605/OSF.IO/WN48Y).
