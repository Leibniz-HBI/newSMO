From April 24 to 26, the Social Media Observatory will host an international symposium on *Indicators of Social Cohesion in Social Media and Online Media*. Registrations are now open. 

<!--more-->

[Register now!](https://events.leibniz-hbi.de/ninja-forms/59ln4s/)

With our initial period currently nearing its end and a subsequent five-year funding period in sight, we are honored to host this event for our fellow social media researchers from all over the world. We are fortunate to welcome a group of internationally renowned speakers who will share with us their contributions on indicators of social cohesion.

The Symposium will kick off on the evening of April 24 with a Context Collapse, an alternative keynote format that encourages networking and sparks discussion amongst the audience.
